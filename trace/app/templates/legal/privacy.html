Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Trace collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Professional Knowledge Retention Platform.

Information We Collect
  • Account data: name, email address, authentication data, subscription status.
  • Content you import: articles, PDFs, highlights, plain text, and metadata such as titles, URLs, and authors.
  • Usage data: app interactions, review events, device/browser information, IP address, and timestamps.
  • Support and communications: messages you send us and responses we provide.
How We Use Your Information
  • Provide and improve the Trace service, including spaced repetition scheduling and dashboards.
  • Process payments and subscription management via Razorpay.
  • Send transactional emails (verification, password reset, billing notices).
  • Prevent abuse, secure accounts, and enforce our Terms.
  • Respond to support requests and product feedback.
AI Processing Disclosure

When you request concept extraction, your content is securely sent to AI for processing. We do not permit AI to use your data for model training. Only the extracted concepts and processing status are stored in Trace.

Data Retention

We retain your content while your account is active. Upon account deletion, we delete your content and personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required for legal or billing records.

Your Rights
  • Access: request a copy of your personal data.
  • Portability: request export of your content and concepts.
  • Deletion: request deletion of your account and data.
  • Correction: request updates to inaccurate information.
  • Objection/Restriction: limit certain processing where legally applicable.
Cookies and Analytics

We use essential cookies for authentication and session security. We rely on privacy-respecting product analytics; we do not use invasive third-party trackers or advertising cookies.

Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted providers for infrastructure (cloud hosting), email delivery (Flask-Mail/SMTP), payments (Razorpay), and AI processing. These providers process data under contractual obligations consistent with this policy.

Security Measures

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest where supported by our infrastructure. Access to production systems is restricted, logged, and periodically reviewed. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt; we never store plaintext passwords.

Data Deletion Process

You may request deletion via support. We verify ownership, queue deletion, and confirm completion. Backups age out according to their lifecycle policies.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact support@traceyourknowledge.com. We respond within 30 days.
